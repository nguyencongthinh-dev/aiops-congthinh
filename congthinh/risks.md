# A6: Risk Register

| Risk Description | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation | Owner |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Alert Translation Failure:** Translating Datadog monitors to PromQL misses a critical edge case, causing a silent failure (no page) during a real incident. | Medium | High | Run Alertmanager in "shadow mode" for 14 days. Compare its firing history against Datadog monitors. Do not cut over until 95% fidelity is proven. | Platform Engineer |
| **Splunk Export Cap Reached:** We fail to export historical compliance logs before the contract 90-day cancellation window because of the 100 GB/day cap. | Low | High | We currently ingest 52 GB/day. We will immediately stop sending *new* operational logs to Splunk in Week 7, freeing the entire 100 GB/day cap for historical export. We will script the export to run exactly at the limit. | Security Ops |
| **OSS Compute Overload:** A massive traffic spike doubles log volume, overwhelming the self-hosted OpenTelemetry/Loki EC2 instances and dropping logs. | Medium | Medium | Configure Auto Scaling Groups (ASG) on the OTel Collector and Loki Ingestor tiers with a target CPU utilization of 60%. | SRE Team |
| **Security Team Rejects Athena:** The audit team struggles to learn standard SQL for Athena and demands to keep Splunk, ruining the cost model. | High | Medium | Assign one Platform Engineer to pair-program with the Security team for 3 days to translate their top 10 Splunk compliance reports into Athena SQL views prior to cut-over. | Platform Lead |
| **Service Graph Blind Spots:** OTel auto-instrumentation fails to capture traces for an edge-case legacy service, breaking the Service Graph in Grafana Tempo. | Medium | Medium | Maintain a Datadog API fallback in Grafana for that specific service while developers manually add OTel spans. | Dev Team Lead |
| **Developer Resistance to PromQL:** Engineers refuse to learn PromQL, leading to a drop in custom dashboard creation and reduced operational maturity. | High | Low | Create a library of 5 standard Grafana dashboard templates (Service Health, DB Health) that cover 80% of use cases without requiring engineers to write custom PromQL. | Platform Lead |
| **Anomaly Detection Cold Start / Drift:** ML models in `vmanomaly` do not have enough historical baseline data, leading to false alarms or missed deviations during the initial rollout. | Medium | Medium | Run `vmanomaly` in "silent dry-run" mode for 14 days to train on production metrics. Keep standard static threshold alerts as a primary fallback during this phase. | SRE Team / Platform Lead |
| **ML Resource Overload:** The Python-based ML training loop (e.g. Prophet, Isolation Forest) consumes excessive CPU/RAM, delaying anomaly score generation. | Low | Low | Isolate the `vmanomaly` process on a dedicated `m5.large` instance, schedule heavy retraining during off-peak hours (midnight), and set docker resource constraints. | Platform Engineer |
| **eBPF Kernel Incompatibility:** Older Linux kernel versions on legacy application hosts block Grafana Beyla from attaching probes. | Medium | High | Audit host Linux kernels in Week 1. Upgrade kernels to >= 5.6 where necessary. Maintain OTel SDK manual instrumentation as a fallback for incompatible hosts. | Platform Lead |
| **Keep Auto-Remediation Loop Storm:** An auto-remediation playbook runs repeatedly on a recurring alert, causing resource exhaustion (e.g. infinite pod restarts). | Low | High | Enforce strict rate limits in Keep to restrict playbooks from running more than once per 15 minutes. Add a circuit breaker that alerts humans if a playbook fails twice. | SRE Team |
| **Redpanda Queue Disk Saturation:** A volumetric log storm fills Redpanda partition buffers, creating lag or saturating SSD storage. | Low | Medium | Configure a strict 6-hour message retention window in Redpanda. Set up disk watermark alerts at 75% capacity to dynamically drop debug logs at the Vector collector layer. | Platform Engineer |
